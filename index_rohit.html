Featured Recent Project

Believable Story Generation using Transformers

Click for GitHub Repo

During my master's practicum, I embarked on an exhilarating journey to generate anime-style stories using transformer models. Leveraging the capabilities of fine-tuned models of GPT-2 and GPT-3, I trained them on my curated anime dataset. This approach was compared against the zero-shot versions of GPT-2, GPT-3, and GPT-4 to discern the nuances in narrative quality and authenticity. For a comprehensive evaluation, I employed automatic metrics like SelfBLEU and BERTScore. Additionally, I conducted a survey to gather human evaluations, ensuring a holistic assessment of the generated stories.

To elevate the storytelling experience, I collaborated with Midjourney to visualize my most compelling story. The outcome was a mesmerizing amalgamation of text and imagery, vividly bringing the narrative to life.

  • Used Jupyter Notebook as it is hosted on the GCP dataproc’s Master Node and the same is accessed via connection gateway.
  • Data available on hive fetched using PySpark engine in the jupyter notebook using PySpark python module. -PySpark data frame is created then using toPandas() method to convert the PySpark dataframe into Pandas dataframe to utilise the full potential of the pandas library.
